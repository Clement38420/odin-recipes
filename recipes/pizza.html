Pizza Dough

Description

Learn how to make pizza dough at home. This is a simple recipe for pizza dough that doesn't involve any special ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 packet of yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of oil

Instructions

  1. Mix the sugar, yeast, and water in a bowl. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the flour, salt, and oil to the bowl. Mix everything together.
  3. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough and add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
